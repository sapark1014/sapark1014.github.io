I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical
Engineering, Czech Technical University in Prague, supported by the CROP
Fellowship (Marie Skłodowska-Curie COFUND Action). 

My expertise spans image analysis, mathematical modeling, and computational algorithms, particularly focusing on biomedical data. My primary interests lie in developing image processing and analysis techniques, as well as advanced methods for analyzing and integrating multidimensional data.
